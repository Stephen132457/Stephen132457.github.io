Welcome to Lochquarry Outdoor Centre

Lochquarry Outdoor Centre is set in acres of land in the heart of the majestic Argyll hills. On our doorstep is not only magnificent scenery but also a breathtaking selection of outdoor and adventurous activities.

Adventure for Everyone

With activities designed to meet the needs of all ages and experiences of young people, Lochquarry truly brings adventure to everyone. Each activity is run under the instruction of one of our highly trained staff, and all safety equipment is provided.

Welcome School Groups!

However, we are eager to expand our client base and welcome more school groups during the week.
